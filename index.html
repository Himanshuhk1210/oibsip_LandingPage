ABOUT US

We are a group of individuals with extensive expertise in cultivating and distributing pomegranates. With years of experience, we have gained profound insights into the characteristics of superior pomegranates and the intricacies of their cultivation. Pomegranates thrive in warm climates with well-drained soil and ample sunlight. Fortunately, our location in regions of India aligns perfectly with these optimal conditions for pomegranate growth. In our region, the climate fosters the development of pomegranates renowned for their exquisite taste, vibrant hues, and succulent juiciness.

VARIETIES

Ruby Pomegranate

This deep red, medium-sized fruit is known for its sweet and tangy flavor. It is mostly grown in regions with warm climates.

Sweet Pomegranate

A small, rounded, very juicy and sweet fruit variety that ripens in late summer to early fall.
